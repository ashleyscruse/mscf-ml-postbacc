# Capstone Overview

The capstone is an individual project (no teams) that takes you from a dataset through a model, evaluation, and deployment, ending in a research poster. See the [Weeks 10 to 12 schedule](../Schedule/weeks-10-12) for the day-by-day plan.

## Timeline

| Week 8 (Thu 7/23) | **Project proposal due** |

## Project Scope

Data, then model, then evaluation, then poster presentation. Capstone datasets and compute can draw on the [NAIRR Pilot](https://nairrpilot.org/).

## Check-in Expectations

**Check-in 2 (Week 10): ML model progress**

- Model(s) selected and rationale
- Initial training results
- Evaluation metrics
- Challenges encountered
- Next steps for optimization

**Check-in 3 (Week 11): tuning and deployment**

- Hyperparameter tuning results
- Final model performance
- Deployment progress (if applicable)
- Poster outline/draft
- Questions for final polish
