# Poster Requirements

Each student presents a research poster at the end of the program. See the [Weeks 10 to 12 schedule](../Schedule/weeks-10-12) for poster workshop and presentation dates.

## Format

- Standard research poster format (36" x 48" or similar)
- Digital submission required

## Sections

1. Title and author
2. Introduction / problem statement
3. Data and methods
4. Results and evaluation
5. Conclusion and future work
6. Acknowledgments (NAIRR, TACC, program sponsors)

## Presentation

- 5 to 7 minute presentation per student
- Q&A from instructors and peers
- Judging for awards (if applicable)
