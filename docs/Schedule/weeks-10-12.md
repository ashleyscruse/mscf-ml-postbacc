## Poster Requirements

### Format
- Standard research poster format (36" x 48" or similar)
- Digital submission required

### Sections
1. **Title & Author**
2. **Introduction/Problem Statement**
3. **Data & Methods**
4. **Results & Evaluation**
5. **Conclusion & Future Work**
6. **Acknowledgments** (NAIRR, TACC, program sponsors)

### Presentation
- 5-7 minute presentation per student
- Q&A from instructors and peers
- Judging for awards (if applicable)
